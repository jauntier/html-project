About the author

Adrian Nderitu is a 17-year-old high school graduate who has played the guitar since the age of 8, and who dabbles in a little bit of programming. He is an anime enthusiast who does not identify as a 'weeb' as this would necessitate an obsession with Japanese culture as a whole rather than only anime, and he believes in the power that the human ears and what is fed into them have over the well-being of mankind.
